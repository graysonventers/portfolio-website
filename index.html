About

Around the start of the pandemic in mid March of 2020 I had already been coming to terms with the fact that my job as a Grip in the film/tv industry, which I had been doing for four years, was not the best choice for my body long term and I felt that my brain had more to offer in terms of function and creativity.


At the end of March 2020 I began learning how the web works. I started with html and css and quickly moved on to Javascript fundamentals. I started at codecademy.com but switched over to Udemy courses thereafter. These courses on Javascript, React, Nodejs and the MERN stack exposed me to the high level process of coding an application from start to finish, back end to front end. I learned how to utilize technology documentation and began learning and solving leetcode.com problems while also continuing on with these courses and projects.


That brings us to present day where I am ready to offer my skills in a professional environment and to continue my path of learning and advancing as it is a never ending journey.
